  This week's takeaway: Anthropic's rapid, multi-front feature expansion — spanning agentic computer control, mobile-to-desktop task delegation, and deeper platform integration — signals that the competitive frontier is moving from model capability to autonomous workflow ownership, forcing technology strategists to urgently reassess tool selection, security posture, and vendor diversification strategies.
